Tracking FAQs

Your tracking number is issued by Coyefreight once your shipment is booked. It appears on your booking confirmation email (format: CYF-XXXXXXXXX) and on your House Bill of Lading or Air Waybill document.
For air freight, tracking updates appear within 1–2 hours of each milestone event. For sea freight, updates occur at key port events (departure, arrival, discharge). Our CoyeTrack™ system syncs with carrier systems every 15 minutes.
For ocean shipments, it's normal not to see updates for multiple days while at sea. For air freight, if there's been no update for more than 24 hours, please contact our Operations team at +256 757 410 388 or operations@coyefreight.com.
