Our Journey

12 Years of Building Trust

From a small freight brokerage to a global logistics powerhouse — here's how we got here.

2012

Founded in New York

James Coye established Coyefreight with a bold vision: to make enterprise logistics accessible to growing businesses worldwide.

2015

First International Office — Rotterdam

Opened our European hub in Rotterdam, the world's largest seaport, establishing our transatlantic trade lane capabilities.

2018

Asia-Pacific Expansion

Launched operations in Shanghai and Singapore, cementing our position on the critical Asia–North America trade corridor.

2021

CoyeTrack™ Platform Launch

Released our proprietary real-time tracking platform, giving clients full visibility over their global shipments at every stage.

2024

150+ Countries, 5,000+ Clients

Surpassed milestone figures, earning recognition as one of the Top 50 Global Logistics Providers by FreightWeek Magazine.
